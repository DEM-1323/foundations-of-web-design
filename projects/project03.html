Project 3:

Designing a Portfolio Website Wireframe

Goal:

Research different portfolio websites, create a wireframe in Figma for your own portfolio website, and select two fonts from Google Fonts to use for this website.

Instructions:

Research Portfolio Websites:

  • Spend some time browsing the web to find examples of portfolio websites.
  • Look at different styles, layouts, and features that you find appealing or effective.
  • Take notes on what you like and dislike about each site, and consider how you might incorporate these elements into your own design.

Setup Your Figma Workspace:

  • Go to Figma and log in to your account.
  • Create a new project for your portfolio website wireframe.

Create a Wireframe:

  • Within your new project, create a new frame by clicking the Frame tool (or press F).
  • Set the dimensions of the frame to a standard desktop resolution, such as 1440x900.

Design Your Portfolio Website Wireframe:

  • Design the wireframe for your portfolio website, including the following sections:
    • Header: Include your name or logo and a navigation menu.
    • About Me: A section to introduce yourself and provide some background information.
    • Projects/Portfolio: A section to showcase your work, with space for images, descriptions, and links.
    • Contact: A section with your contact information and/or a contact form.
    • Footer: A full-width section for additional links or information.
  • Use Figma’s tools to create the structure and layout of each section.
  • Add placeholder text and shapes to represent images, navigation links, and content areas.
  • Include annotations to explain the purpose of each section and any specific design choices.

Select Fonts from Google Fonts:

  • Visit Google Fonts.
  • Browse the available fonts and select two fonts that you would like to use for your portfolio website.
  • Make a note of the font names and consider how you will use them (e.g., one for headings and one for body text).

Viewing Your Wireframe:

  • Once you have completed your wireframe, use Figma’s presentation mode to view and present your design.
  • Click the “Present” button in the top right corner of Figma to view your wireframe as a full-screen presentation.
